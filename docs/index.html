Business English Class are planning a special event called GEC Market day*.
This event will help us learn more about business and will also help a charity we care about, the Startlight Children’s Foundation.
On the GEC Market Day, we will have different stalls selling food and drink, and donated items, and we will have raffle, and photo booth including other activities to raise money. All the resources we collect will go to Startlight Children’s Foundation to support their good work in helping children face the fear, pain, and stress of serious illness.

You can help us given a hope of happiness for a child!
