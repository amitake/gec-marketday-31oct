we have a special event called GEC Market day.
This event will help us learn more about business and will also help a charity we care about, the Startlight Children’s Foundation.

What Startlight Children’s Foundation do?

They work in partnership with health professionals to bring the fun, joy and laughter that is essential to sick kids’ health and wellbeing.
